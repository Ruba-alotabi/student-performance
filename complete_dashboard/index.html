Key Findings

  • Regression Analysis

    Regression models performed poorly with R² score of -0.0075, indicating no meaningful linear relationship between study hours, sleep patterns, and student scores.

  • Classification Analysis

    Classification accuracy peaked at 41.5% using Gradient Boosting, with quiz scores and assignment completion rates as the most predictive features.

  • Clustering Analysis

    K-Means clustering achieved a silhouette score of 0.18, identifying three distinct student groups: high performers (32%), average performers (45%), and struggling students (23%).

  • Anomaly Detection

    Isolation Forest detected 129 anomalies (2.58% of students), primarily characterized by unusual combinations of high stress and high performance.
